Ryan Sypkens

Coach and Mentor

Ryan Sypkens is a Sacramento native who has an immense passion for the game of basketball. Ryan is a shooting specialist and expert in shooting mechanics and training, as well as general basketball training. In late 2015, he launched his training company, Syp’s T.O.U.C.H. Shooting Academy, and has since been training aspiring basketball student-athletes and professionals.

Experience

Graduating from Franklin High School of Elk Grove in 2009, he was selected to the Sacramento All-Metro team before his team fell short to McClymonds high school of Oakland in the state playoffs.

Directly following his high-school career, he enrolled into UC Davis and worked his way into the starting line-up a few games into freshman season. He then went on to break the single-game, single-season, and career 3 point record at UC Davis as well as the Big West Conference career three point record. In addition, he finished #2 in 3 point percentage in all of NCAA D1 as well as top 5 in 3 pointers made per game respectively. Following his career at Davis, he went on to play professionally in South America, Canada and Tokyo, Japan.
