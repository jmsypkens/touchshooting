Skills Package (Individual-Group)

Skills package focuses on developing and/or sharpening fundamentals and building a SHIFT package on top. The first step is to evaluate the player’s strengths and weaknesses and develop a comprehensive move and skill (SHIFT) package that best fits his or her size, frame, and basketball trends.

Skills Package (Individual)

Touch Shooting package focus specifically on the art of shooting a basketball. This package includes breaking down the player’s form and rebuilding the shot from the ground up. Once that is complete, we will use the T.O.U.C.H. Shooters philosophy to develop an individualized program.
